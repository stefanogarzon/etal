Tools

Bulk maintenance operations and app updates.

Library & sync

Library folder: …. To use it on another computer, keep this folder in a synced service (e.g. Google Drive) and click “Open / switch library” there. Don't run the app on two machines at once — let the sync finish first, to avoid database conflicts.

Refresh metadata

Re-query CrossRef for every article that has a DOI. Useful after corrections in CrossRef or to fill missing fields.

Reclassify topics (keyword)

Re-run the offline keyword matcher on every article. Preview the proposed moves before applying.

Reclassify with AI

Re-run AI classification over articles already in the library — handy after a bulk import that hit a free-tier limit and left papers in _uncategorized. Runs in the background, paced to your Requests/min, retrying on rate-limit. Review the proposed moves, then apply.

AI assist — optional

With an API key, Et al. suggests a topic for each paper (and can propose new ones) and writes short summaries. It's off until you add a key; without one, the app classifies by keywords. Works with any OpenAI-compatible provider — Groq, Google Gemini (free), a local Ollama, OpenRouter, … Any failed call falls back to keyword matching.
